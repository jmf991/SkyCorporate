Approach

At Sky we want to build a business that is durable for the long-term. This means acting in the interests of our customers, our people, our partners and the communities in which we live and work. It is a core part of our Believe in better ethos - our commitment to constant renewal and improvement in everything that we do. It's through this approach that we achieve sustainable success.
